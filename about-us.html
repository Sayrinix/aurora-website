Aurora Aksnes

Aurora was born on June 15, 1996, in Stavanger, Norway, and grew up in the forests and fjords near Bergen, which shaped her intimate connection with nature. She demonstrated artistic talent as a child: she wrote songs from the age of six and studied dance and piano. An emotionally sensitive person, she has spoken openly about her introversion, social anxiety, and personal healing through her art, turning pain into hope in her music.
