Infections of a
Different Kind (Step 1)

Description

A more experimental and political release, this EP-album hybrid explores themes of connection, activism, and identity. Aurora's sound becomes more layered and bold, with tracks like Queendom and Forgotten Love celebrating empowerment and vulnerability.

Influences

She embraced bolder electronic elements while staying connected to her environmental and humanitarian themes. Inspired by political unrest and a need for collective healing, the album reflects influences from alternative synth-pop and artists like Lorde and Imogen Heap.
