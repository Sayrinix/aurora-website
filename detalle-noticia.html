Aurora Announces New Album "My Soft Machine Part II"

Norwegian alt-pop artist AURORA has officially announced the release of her highly anticipated new album, My Soft Machine Part II, set to arrive this fall. Following the critical success of her previous works, this second installment promises to dive even deeper into the emotional and philosophical themes that have defined her music so far. The announcement was made through a heartfelt video on her social media, accompanied by a poetic teaser featuring atmospheric visuals and nature imagery.

"My Soft Machine Part II is a continuation of my inner world," AURORA explained. "It’s about the parts of ourselves that we hide, protect, and sometimes fear. I wanted this album to feel like a warm, strange dream — the kind that teaches you something about yourself when you wake up." Fans can expect a mix of haunting ballads, experimental soundscapes, and her signature ethereal vocals that have captivated audiences worldwide.

Alongside the announcement, Aurora revealed that the first single, titled Ghost in the Garden, will drop next month. She has also hinted at exciting collaborations and new sonic directions. The album is expected to explore contrasting textures — both delicate and chaotic — reflecting themes of transformation, vulnerability, and self-discovery. Longtime listeners will likely find familiar lyrical depth, but with an evolved sound palette that continues to push boundaries.

Aurora will support the album with a world tour beginning early next year. Known for her deeply immersive live performances, she stated that this tour will include new visual elements inspired by nature, mythology, and dreams. "It’s not just music — it’s a world I want to share with you," she told her fans. With this new chapter, AURORA continues to establish herself as one of the most visionary and emotionally resonant artists in modern pop.
